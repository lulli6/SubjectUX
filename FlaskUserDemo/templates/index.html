Year 10 Option Choices

All students in year 10 will learn the compulsory one-year course of:

  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Physical Education and Health

    • All students in year 10 learn the compulsory half year course of Social Science.

    Options: Year 10 students also choose from a range of subjects in two OR three other learning areas, according to their personal interest.

  • Technology
  • The Arts
  • Languages

    • Languages are full year courses. If choosing a language student will select only one more course from a different learning area. Māori is also offered as a half year course. If selecting the half year Māori option, students should select two more courses from either Arts or Technology.
